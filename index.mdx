---

The Multitaper Toolbox provides scientifically rigorous spectral estimation for time-series data using the multitaper method — the gold standard for reducing spectral variance in EEG, neural recordings, and physiological signals. Originally developed for sleep neurophysiology research at the Prerau Lab, it is available in Python, MATLAB, and R with identical parameter interfaces across all three implementations.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Superior spectral estimation" icon="chart-line">
    DPSS tapers produce spectra with lower variance than single-taper or periodogram methods, making weak oscillations visible in noisy signals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flexible taper weighting" icon="weight-scale">
    Choose from unity, eigenvalue, or adaptive weighting to balance spectral resolution and leakage suppression.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Parallel processing" icon="microchip">
    All three implementations support multiprocessing for fast computation over long recordings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-language consistency" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Python, MATLAB, and R results agree to within 10⁻⁹, so you can switch languages without changing your science.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
